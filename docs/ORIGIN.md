# Origin

Property operations are not just a leasing calendar problem. They are an evidence, obligation, and packet-trust problem. The moment an insurance certificate expires, a CAM schedule drifts, or an option notice points at the wrong commencement date, teams need a surface that maps renewal pressure to the next safe move.

We built `lease-obligation-risk-board` to make that operating layer explicit. The point is not to replace a lease system or document vault. The point is to show what a PropTech / Real Estate operator surface should look like when the audience needs to manage renewals, tenant obligations, and landlord packet posture with real commercial consequences attached.
